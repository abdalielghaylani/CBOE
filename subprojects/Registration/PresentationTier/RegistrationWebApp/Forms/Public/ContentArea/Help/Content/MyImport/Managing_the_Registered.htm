Managing the Registered Records

When you register a record to the permanent table, the record is registered as a batch. You can search, edit, and add batches to the registered records. Once a batch is added, you can edit, move or delete the batches added.
Registration lets you search for records based on any database field. Make your search criteria as specific as possible to minimize the list of search results. Alternatively, try broadening your search criteria to locate the records you want.
Tip: After searching, mark the search results for later review. See "Marking records" for information.

Searching for registered compounds

To search the registered compounds in the permanent table:
1. Click Search Registry option in the Registration home page. The Search Permanent Registry page appears.

 

2. Do one of the following:
To return all records, click Retrieve All.
To search for specific records, fill in the search fields to specify you search criteria and click Search. For more information on search types, see "Types of searches".

Approving a registered record

You can approve a registered record to be valid.
To approve a record:
Mark the record.
Click Approve Marked. A page displaying operation sucess message appears.

Editing a registered record

You can modify the record and save it.
To edit a record:
1. Click Reg Number link corresponding to the record. The Registry Record page appears.
2. Click Edit and make the required changes.
3. Click Submit to save the changes made.
Deleting a registered record
You can also delete a registered record.
To delete a record, do one of the following:
Mark the record and click Delete Marked.
Click Reg Number link corresponding to the record. The Registry Record page appears. Click Delete Record.

Send records to Inventory

If Registration Enterprise is integrated with Inventory Enterprise, you can send hits from your search in Registration system to the Inventory sytem. This eliminates the need to enter the same information the again in Inventory system.
To send registered records to Inventory:
Click Reg Number link corresponding to the record. The Registry Record page appears.
Click Send to Inventory.
