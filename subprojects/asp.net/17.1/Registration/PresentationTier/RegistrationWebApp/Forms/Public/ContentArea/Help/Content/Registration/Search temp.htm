Managing temporary records

You can search for records in the temporary table, edit records, and delete records. If you are a registrar, you can also register records to the permanent table.

Searching records in the temporary table

You can either retrieve all the records in the temporary table or only specific records. You can search by structure, temporary ID, or by any other parameter.
To search the temporary table for a record:
1. Click Search Temp in the Registration Enterprise home page. The Search Temporary Records page appears.

 

2. Do one of the following:
To retrieve all records in the temporary table, click Retrieve All.
To retrieve records that meet specific criteria, enter the search criteria under Component information or Batch Information and click Search. A list of records matching the search criteria you specified appears.
To create a hitlist of all the records displayed, click Mark All. The count of the marked hits appears on the right side of the page.
To deselect all the selected records, click Un- Mark All. For more information on marking records, "Marking records".

Editing records in the temporary table

You can edit the records in the temporary table before they are registered in the permanent table.
Note: Editing cannot be undone.
To edit a record in the temporary table:
1. Search the temporary table for the record to edit.
2. In the results list, click Review record corresponding to the record. The Temporary Records page appears.
3. Click Edit.
4. Make the required changes in the registry, component, or batch information and click Submit to save the changes or click Cancel to undo any unsaved changes.

Deleting records from the temporary table

If you have the appropriate privileges, you can delete a record from the temporary table.
Note: Deleted records cannot be recovered.
To delete a record from the temporary table:
1. Search the temporary table for the record to delete.
2. Mark the record to delete.
3. Click Delete Marked. The Delete Records page appears.
4. Add a description for the records to be deleted.
5. Click Delete and then Done to delete the records or click Cancel if you do not want to delete the record.
6. Click Home to go to the registration Enterprise home page.
