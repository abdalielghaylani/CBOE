Direct register

If you have Admin privilege, you can use this option to bypass the temporary table and submit a record directly to the permanent table.
To register directly to the permanent table:
1. Fill in the required fields in the Submit New Record form.
2. Click Register. All unique records are registered and Registry number is generated. If duplicate records exists, the duplicate checking page appears:

 

You can register the duplicate either as a duplicate record or as a new batch by clicking “Add Batch” or “Duplicate”. The record is registered and the Registry number is generated.
3. Click Done.
