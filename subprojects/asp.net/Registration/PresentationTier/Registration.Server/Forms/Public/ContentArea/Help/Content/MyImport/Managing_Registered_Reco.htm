Managing Registered Records

When you register a record to the permanent table, the record is registered as a batch. You can search, edit, and add batches to the registered records. Once a batch is added, you can edit, move or delete the batches added.
Registration lets you search for records based on any database field. Make your search criteria as specific as possible to minimize the list of search results. Alternatively, try broadening your search criteria to locate the records you want.
After searching, mark the search results for later review. See "Marking records" for information.
