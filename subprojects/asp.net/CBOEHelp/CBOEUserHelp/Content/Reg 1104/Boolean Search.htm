Boolean Search

Users may perform boolean search using boolean operators AND, OR, and NOT. The following table lists a few examples of boolean search.

Operator

Criteria

Records retrieved

AND

Ben* AND *bromo*

Records that contain on the same field benzofuran and bromobenzene.

OR

Ben* OR *bromo*

Records that contain benzofuran or bromobenzene.

NOT

Ben* NOT *bromo*

Records containing benzofuran but not bromobenzene.
