Deleting a Registered Record

If the user has adequate privileges, he/she can delete a registered record.

To delete a record, do one of the following:

  1. Mark the record and click Delete Marked.

Alternatively,

  1. Click Reg Number link corresponding to the record.
  2. The Registry Record page appears.
  3. Click Delete Record.
