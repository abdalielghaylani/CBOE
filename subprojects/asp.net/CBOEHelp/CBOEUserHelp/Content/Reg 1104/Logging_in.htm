Logging in

To open the Registration Enterprise home page:
1. Enter your user name and password in the ChemBioOffice Enterprise login page and click Log in.
2. In the ChemBioOffice Entereprise Manager home page, click Main Menu under Registration Enterprise. The home page of Registration Enterprise appears.
