Editing a registered record

It is possible to edit a registered record and save it:

To edit a record:

  1. After searching for registered records, the list of results displays.
  2. Click on the "Reg Number" link corresponding to the record. The Registry Record page appears.

  1. Click Edit and make the required changes.

  1. Click Save to save the changes made.
