Add Compound from ACX system to a Reaction Drawing

To add a compound from ChemACX to the reaction drawing:

  1. After executing the search, select a substance from the "Show Results" tab
  2. Click on the “Add” button, the following menu item displays:
  • Left of Arrow as Reactant
  • Above Arrow as Reagent
  • Right of Arrow as Product
  • To Grid as Reagent

  1. The Chemical Structure from the add-in will be added to the Reaction field of the "Reaction" section accordingly. A new entry in the Reactants or products table also appears in the "Reaction" section.
