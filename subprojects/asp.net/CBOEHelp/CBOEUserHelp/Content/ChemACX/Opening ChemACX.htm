Opening ChemACX

To open a ChemACX database:

  1. Enter https://servername/COEManager into your web browser address bar. <servername> is the name of the server where ChemBioOffice Enterprise is installed. The ChemBioOffice Enterprise log in page appears.
  2. Enter a valid username and password.
  3. Click the Log in button to display the ChemBioOffice Enterprise home page.
  4. Click on the Search button within the ChemACX section in the ChemBioOffice Enterprise home page. The ChemACX search form appears
