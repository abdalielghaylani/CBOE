Quick Permanent Registration

Quick Permanent Registration gathers information and attempts to permanently register the samples in bulk into the Registration System. Upon successful registration, a reg number and batch number are returned to the Samples table in the Reaction/Process Reaction section and to the Properties table in Sample Information section.

To register a sample permanently:

  1. Create a sample and assign products to it.
  2. Select the samples to register, click on the Registration form tool, and select Quick Permanent Registration.

The Registration dialog appears for confirmation.

  1. Click Yes. The Registration dialog displays the registration results.

  1. Click OK. The reg number and batch number are returned to the Samples table in the Reaction section.

  1. Navigate to the Samples section, the same reg number and batch number are populated in the Sample Information SM>>Properties table and correspond to the selected samples.
