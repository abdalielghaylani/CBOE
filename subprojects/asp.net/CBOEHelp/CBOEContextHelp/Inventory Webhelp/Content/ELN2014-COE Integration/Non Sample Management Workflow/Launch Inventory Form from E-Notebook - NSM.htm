Launch Inventory Form from E-Notebook - NSM

You can view the contents of an Inventory container if a valid barcode is available.

Note: Ensure the Barcode column has a valid barcode prior to launching the Inventory form.

To launch the Inventory form:

  1. Press the <Ctrl> key and click the barcode value that appears as a reference in the Barcode column, as shown below,

An Inventory form is launched and displays the container details of the selected barcode, as shown below.

Note: Entering a valid barcode in the Reactants table does not retrieve the container details from Inventory. It is not supported in the Reaction W COE Integration section, it is only supported in the Materials and Prep section.

The following screen shot shows a valid barcode entered in the second row, with no container details fetched from Inventory system, however you can still launch the corresponding container details in Inventory by pressing the <Ctrl> key + clicking on the barcode value in the Reactants table.
