Registration Push

It is possible to register a compound in Registration Enterprise through E-Notebook application.

Note: for detailed information about the E-Notebook workflow and the steps listed below, please refer to the E-Notebook user's guide by clicking on Help>>E-Notebook Guide within the E-Notebook application.

To register samples in Bulk from E-Notebook application:

  1. Log in to E-Notebook
  2. Create a Notebook and add an Experiment
  3. Fill in the Title section and add "Reaction" section to your experiment
  4. Draw a reaction in the reaction field and save
  5. Create Samples, by clicking on Create Samples button

  1. After creating samples and assigning products to it, click on the "Sample Registration" form tool and select one of the following options:
  • Reg Temp
  • Reg Perm
