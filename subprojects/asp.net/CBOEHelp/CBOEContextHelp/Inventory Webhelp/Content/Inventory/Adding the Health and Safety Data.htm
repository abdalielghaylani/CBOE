Adding the Health and Safety Data

You can assign corresponding health and safety data to each substance you manage. This data is standard, comprehensible worldwide, and necessary to regulate the health and safety of all workers, and scientists, handling chemicals, or any type of material.

Notes:

  • To manage substances (Add, Edit, etc.) and add health and safety data, you must have "INV_MANAGE_SUBSTANCES" and "INV_SUBSTANCE_SAFETY_DATA" privileges.
  • The administrator has to set DISPLAY_SAFETY_DATA= True in the invconfig.ini file to enable this feature. Please refer to Enable Safety Data for more details.
  • It is not possible to add Health and Safety data to a compound linked to the Registration application.

To add the Health and Safety Data:

  1. From Inventory Enterprise, click on Manage Substances.

  1. Click Add Mode.
  2. Draw a structure and enter its corresponding attributes: Substance Name, CAS Registry #, and ACX Number.

  1. Click OK. A new substance is created.

  1. Go to ChemBioOffice Enterprise home page, click on Manage Substances link and search for the newly-added substance.
  2. Click Edit Mode.

  1. The Edit substance attributes window appears.
  2. From the Hazards tab, check the boxes that correspond with the hazards for this substance.

  1. Click OK.
  2. The Existing Substance has been Updated window appears and displays the list of selected hazards and precautions.

  1. Click OK.
  2. When you add the example substance above to a container, its health and safety data displays under the Substance tab as shown below.

  1. To edit the health and safety data, click on the Manage Substance link under Substance tab, and make your modifications.
  2. Click OK.

Safety Data Audit Trail

The Analyze Audit Trail link in the Administrative Menu lets you keep track of the actions performed on the containers, locations, and compounds in Inventory Enterprise. Additionally, Audit Trail lets you track the actions performed on the compounds at the Health and Safety data level.

Please refer to Analyze Audit Trail section under Inventory Tasks for more details on how to audit data.

Note: To audit the safety data, specify the Container ID in the Audit Report window.
