Floating windows

You can move the Record Toolbar, navigation pane, and form editing Properties panes to where ever you need them. To move a window, click+drag its title bar to the new location.

Organizing the Navigation Pane

displaying/hiding buttons

To display or hide the slider bar buttons, click the right-arrow icon at the bottom of the navigation pane and select an option.

 

sliding the divider bar

To adjust the size of the navigation pane, click+drag the divider bar resize handle.
