Full Structure Searching

Full structure searching retrieves structures as drawn, with no additional attachments. Isotopes are recognized as hits.

To perform an full structure search:

  1. On the Results Form, click New Query.
    A blank Search form appears.
  2. Draw a structure.
  3. From the Search Type list, select Full Structure.
     
  4. Click Search.
    Three records including isotopes are retrieved.
