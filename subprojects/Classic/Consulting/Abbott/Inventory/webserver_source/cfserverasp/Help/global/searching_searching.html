Performing a Simple Search

To perform a Simple Search:

  1. Login to the Sample Database.
  2. Enter "benz" without the quotation marks in the Molecule Name field.
  3. Click Search.
    The results of the search appear in a Results Form.
    Search results are displayed according to the Search Preferences dialog box.
