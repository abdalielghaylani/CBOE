Restoring Hit Lists

Restore Hit List allows user to restore a hit list which was previously generated.

There are two options when restoring a hit list:

  • Restore the last hit list generated
  • Restore one of the saved hit lists    

To Restore the Last Hit List generated:

  • Select Restore > Last from the Hit Lists Menu.
    The hit list is displayed in list form.

To Restore a Saved Hit List:

  1. Select Restore > Saved from the Hit Lists Menu.
     
  2. Select the appropriate hit list from the listbox.
  3. Click  Restore.
    The hit list is displayed in list form.

For more information about hit lists, please see Managing Hit Lists.
