Identity Searching

Identity searching retrieves only structures exactly as drawn (taking stereochemistry into account).  Isotopes are not recognized as hits.   

To perform an identity search:

  1. On the Results Form, click New Query.
    A blank Search form appears.
  2. Draw a structure.
  3. From the Search Type list, select Identity.

  4. Click Search.
