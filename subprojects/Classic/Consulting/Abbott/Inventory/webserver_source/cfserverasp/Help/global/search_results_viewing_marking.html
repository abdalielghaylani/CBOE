Marking Records

Records can be marked (added to a progressive hit list) from both Details and List View. Marked hits are user specific and can be retrieved at any time by clicking Show Marked.

To mark a record:

  • In List View: In the table cell of the record you want to mark, click Mark Record.
    The record is marked and the button changes to Unmark Record.

     
  • In Details View: In the upper left hand corner of the record, click Mark Record.
    The record is marked and the button changes to Unmark Record.


      

To remove the record from the marked records list:

  • Click Unmark Record. 

To view the marked records:

  • Click Show Marked in the left frame of the page.
    The marked records are loaded into the current hit list and displayed.

To delete marked records:

  • Click Clear Marked.
    The marked records are deleted.
