Excluding Special Locations

You can limit your search by excluding special locations, such as Trash Can, Disposed, and On Order, from the search. This reduces the size of the search result list and saves the efforts required to locate the desired result in the search result list.

To specify which special locations are to be excluded from the search :

  1. Click the Special link. The Special Inventory Locations dialog box appears:
  1. Select the check box next to the special locations that are to be excluded from the search.
  2. Click the OK button.

Note: If you want to add more locations to the list of special locations, please see your system administrator.
