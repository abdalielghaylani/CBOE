Due Date

Type of field: Date

This field is meant to hold the date by which the ordered container needs to be delivered. To specify date in this field, either type the date in the Due Date text box or click the icon next to the Due Date text box to display a calendar. Select the required date of the required year and month from the calendar, the selected date is inserted into the Due Date text box.
