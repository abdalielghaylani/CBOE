Purity

Type of field: Number

This field is meant to hold purity of a mixture. Purity is a calculation reflecting how much of a mixture is purely the substance recorded. You can specify any of the following units for purity: microgram/mililiter, micromolar, mili molar, molar, normal, parts per million, and percent. To specify unit for the purity, select the appropriate unit from the list box to the right of the Purity text box.
