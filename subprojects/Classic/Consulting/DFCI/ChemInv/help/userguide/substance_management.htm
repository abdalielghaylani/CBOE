Substance Management

The Inventory substances can be managed using the Substance Management area of Inventory Enterprise. The Substance Management area can be accessed by clicking the Manage Substances link within the Inventory Enterprise section in the home page of ChemOffice Enterprise or by clicking the Manage Substances link in the menu bar of the Inventory Enterprise home page. The following figure displays the Substance Management area:

In the Substance Management area, you can create new substances and search for existing substances.

Note: You can create a new substance only if you have sufficient privileges to do so. For information about the privileges associated with the Inventory Enterprise roles, please see Privileges.
