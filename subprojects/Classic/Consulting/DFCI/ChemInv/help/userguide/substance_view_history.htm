Viewing History

The history of a substance allows you to keep track of the actions performed on the substance in Inventory Enterprise. You can view the history of a substance using the audit reports. You can also filter the actions listed in the audit report of a substance to gather information only about the specific actions.

To allow you to view substance history, Inventory Enterprise provides two types of audit reports, which are as follows:

  • Standard audit report
  • Aggregate audit report
