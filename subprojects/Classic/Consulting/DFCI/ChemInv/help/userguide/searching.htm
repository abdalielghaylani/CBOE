Searching Inventory Enterprise

The searching feature of Inventory Enterprise enables you to locate a substance, container, or plate stored in the Inventory Enterprise database.

In Inventory Enterprise, different types of users can perform different types of searches according to their requirements. For example, chemists need to search Inventory Enterprise on the basis of attributes, such as chemical structure, substance name, and CAS number. They can perform the search with the help of the substructure search. Similarly, stock room or receiving room personnel can use the advanced search to search Inventory Enterprise on the basis of attributes, such as container and location barcode.
