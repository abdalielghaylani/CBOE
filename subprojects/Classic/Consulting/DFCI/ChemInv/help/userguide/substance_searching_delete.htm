Deleting a Substance

To delete an Inventory Enterprise substance:

  1. Perform a search in the Substance Management area of Inventory Enterprise to search the substance that is to be deleted. For information about searching substances in Inventory Enterprise, please see Searching for a Substance.
  2. Click the Details tab corresponding to the appropriate substance. A page containing the details, such as molecular weight and molecular formula of the substance appears.
  3. Click the Delete Record button. The following page appears:

  1. Click the OK button to confirm the deletion. A page informing you that the substance is deleted from Inventory Enterprise appears.
  2. Click the OK button.
