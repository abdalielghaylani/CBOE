ACX Number

Field Type: Text

This field is meant to hold ACX number, which is a unique identifier and registry number for the substances stored in ChemACX and ChemFinder, and the substances submitted by Open Chemistry. ACX number can store information relating to the vendor, quality, package size, and physical material of the substances.
