Registering ChemACX Chemicals or Creating Inventory Containers

You can register the ChemACX chemicals in Registration Enterprise or send the ChemACX chemicals to Inventory Enterprise, if Registration Enterprise and Inventory Enterprise are installed and ChemACX is configured appropriately.

Note: For information about configuring ChemACX for integrating it with Registration Enterprise and/or Inventory Enterprise, please see your system administrator.

What would you like to do?

  • Register a ChemACX chemical in Registration Enterprise
  • Send a ChemACX chemical to Inventory Enterprise
