Details View

By clicking the Details button in List view, you can view the search results in the Details view, as shown in the following figure:

Details view provides you all the details of a chemical, such as molecular formula, molecular weight, and CAS number of the chemical. In the Details view, you can perform various tasks, which are as follows:

  • Zoom-in on the structure of a chemical by clicking the following icon:
  • View the synonyms of a chemical by clicking the following icon:
  • View the search results in the List view by clicking the following icon:
  • Mark a chemical record for later review by clicking the following icon:
  • Register a chemical in Registration Enterprise
  • Access the MSDX data for a chemical
  • Browse vendor related information
  • Add products to your shopping cart
