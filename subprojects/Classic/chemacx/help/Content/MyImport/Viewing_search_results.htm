Viewing search results

You can browse your search results in either List View or Details View. To help you, both views offer several features::
Click the icon: to zoom-in on the structure of a chemical.
Click the icon: to view the synonyms of a chemical.
Click the icon: to view the search results in the List view.
Click the icon: to mark a chemical record for later review.
Register a chemical in Registration Enterprise.
Access the MSDX data for a chemical.
Browse vendor information.
Add products to your shopping cart.

Browsing vendor information

To browse the vendor information in the Details view:
1. Click a tab:
ALL VENDORS: Lists all the vendors in ChemACX.
YOUR FAVORITE: Lists only the favorite vendors.
2. Select the vendor from the list. The catalogs associated with the selected vendor appears next to the vendors list, as shown below:

:

3. Hold down the CTRL key and select multiple vendors to compare catalogs.
4. Click CATALOG DETAILS corresponding to a catalog. A description of the chemical from the vendor's catalog appears.:

5. Click Close.
6. Click COMPACT VIEW to view the catalogs in the Compact view, as shown below

:

By default, the catalogs appear in the Catalog view. To return to the Catalog view, click the CATALOG VIEW tab.
In the Details view, you can view the shopping cart and add products to it.. See "Using the shopping cart".

Accessing MSDX data

Note: If MSDX data is not available for a chemical record, the MSDX link for the record is disabled.
