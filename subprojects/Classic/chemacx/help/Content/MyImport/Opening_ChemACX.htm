Opening ChemACX

1. To open ChemACX, type http://servername into your Web browser. The main ChemOffice Enterprise window appears.
Note: Servername is the name of the ChemBioOffice Enterprise sever. For more information about your server, contact your system administrator.
2. Log into ChemBioOffice Enterprise.
3. Click ChemACX within the Chemical Databases section in the ChemBioOffice Enterprise home page. The ChemACX search form appears:
