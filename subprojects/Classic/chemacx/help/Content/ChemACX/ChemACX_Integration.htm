ChemACX integration

You can register the ChemACX chemicals in Registration Enterprise or send the ChemACX chemicals to Inventory Enterprise, if Registration Enterprise and Inventory Enterprise are installed and ChemACX is configured appropriately.
Note: For Help integrating ChemACX with either Registration Enterprise or Inventory Enterprise, see your system administrator.

Registering ChemACX chemicals

To register a ChemACX chemical in Registration Enterprise:
1. Search the required chemical in ChemACX.
2. Click Register in the compound record. The Register a compound dialog box appears

:

3. Click OK. The chemical is registered.

:

Creating inventory containers

To create containers in Inventory Enterprise from ChemACX chemicals:
1. Open the shopping cart.
2. Click Send to Inventory Manager. The Create Inventory Containers from ChemACX page appears

:

Consult your system administrator, if you do not see the Send to Inventory Manager button.
3. Provide the required information and click OK. This page appears

:

4. Click Commit. This page appears

:

5. Click View to view the new containers.
