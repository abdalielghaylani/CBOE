Batch attributes:

CAS Registry Number: This text field holds CAS registry number, which is a unique accession number assigned by the Chemical Abstracts Service, a division of the American Chemical Society. This field ensures uniqueness of a compound and is assigned to every uniquely-identifiable compound.
Molecular Formula: This text field holds molecular formula of a substance. The molecular formula is a method of describing a substance's makeup on atomic level.
Catalog Number: This field holds the catalog number.
