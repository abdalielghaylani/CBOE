Ordering the chemicals

The chemicals stored in the shopping cart can be ordered in various ways, which are as follows:

Exporting the chemicals to MS Excel

Exporting the chemicals to MS Word template, or

Printing the shopping cart

Note: To export the chemicals to MS Excel or MS Word, you need to make changes to the .ini files of ChemACX. Please consult your system administrator for this purpose.
