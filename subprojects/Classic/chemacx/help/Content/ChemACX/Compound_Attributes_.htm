Compound attributes:

Substance Name: This text field holds the name of the substance.
ACX Number: This text field holds ACX number, which is a unique identifier and registry number for the substances stored in ChemACX and ChemFinder, and the substances submitted by Open Chemistry. ACX number can store information relating to the vendor, quality, package size, and physical material of the substances.
MolWeight Range: This numeric field holds the molecular weight range of the substance. Molecular weight is the average mass of a molecule of a substance.
Substructure: This structure field holds the structure or substructure of a substance. The structure of a substance is drawn using the ChemDraw plug-in. For information about drawing structures using ChemDraw plug-in, please see ChemDraw Quick Reference Guide.
