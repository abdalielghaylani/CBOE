Adding Items to the shopping cart

To add items to the shopping cart:

1. Search the chemical that is to be added to the shopping cart and view it in the Details view.
2. Browse through the vendors related information and select the appropriate vendor to display its catalog, as shown in the following figure:

3. Select add to cart next to the required chemicals and specify the desired quantity in the QTY text box. The selected chemicals are added to the shopping cart.
