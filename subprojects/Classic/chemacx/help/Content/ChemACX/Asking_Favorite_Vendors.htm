Asking Favorite Vendors to Participate in ChemACX

CambridgeSoft keeps on adding catalogs of new suppliers to ChemACX. Therefore, the supplier you are looking for might be there in the next release of ChemACX, or it might already be in the CambridgeSoft’s WWW subscription service, ChemInfo Pro.

However, you can also ask your favorite supplier to participate in ChemACX so that you can surely find your favorite supplier in the next release of ChemACX. To participate, supplier needs to send its product list to CambridgeSoft. The preferred format for sending the product list is a ChemFinder database containing structure details of the products. Other electronic formats, such as Access databases and Excel spreadsheets can also be used for sending the product list. If the product list is small in size, it can be sent in the form of brochure. The supplier can either e-mail the product list to csinfo@cambridgesoft.com or send it to the following address:

ChemACX Data, CambridgeSoft Corporation, 100 CambridgePark Drive, Cambridge, MA 02140 (USA)

To view a list of the vendors already there in ChemACX, please see the “Participating Vendors” page in the ChemACX Users Guide.
