Exporting to MS-Word template

To export the chemicals to the MS-Word template:

1. Open the shopping cart.
2. Click Export to MS-Word Template. The ChemACX Requisitioner Information form appears:

3. Provide the required information in the form and click Export to MS-Word Template. A link using which you can download the Word file appears.
4. Click the link to open or save the Excel file.
