Using the shopping cart

You can use the shopping cart to save the list of the chemicals to order.
To view the shopping cart, click Shopping Cart in either List view or Details view.
This figure appears:

:

Adding Items

To add items to the shopping cart:
1. Search for the chemical to add and view it in the Details view.
2. Browse through the vendors information and select the appropriate vendor to display its catalog.

:

3. Select add to cart next to the required chemicals and enter the quantity in the QTY text box. The selected chemicals are added to the shopping cart.

Removing Items

To remove an item from your shopping cart:
1. Open the shopping cart. Click REMOVE next to the item or click Remove All to empty the shopping cart.

Ordering chemicals

The chemicals stored in the shopping cart can be ordered in various ways:

Exporting the chemicals to MS Excel

Exporting the chemicals to MS Word template, or

Printing the shopping cart

Note: To export the chemicals to MS Excel or MS Word, you need to edit the .ini files of ChemACX. Consult your system administrator.

Exporting the chemicals to Excel

To export the chemicals to Excel:
1. Open the shopping cart.
2. Click Export to Excel. The Requisitioner Information form appears

:

3. Provide the required information in the form and click Export to Excel. A link to download the Excel file appears.
4. Click the link to open or save the Excel file.

Exporting to a Word template

To export the chemicals to a Word template:
1. Open the shopping cart.
2. Click Export to MS-Word Template. The ChemACX Requisitioner Information form appears

:

3. Provide the required information in the form and click Export to MS-Word Template. A link to download the Word file appears.
4. Click the link to open or save the Excel file.

Printing the shopping cart

To print the shopping cart:
1. Open the shopping cart.
2. Click Print.
3. In the Print dialog box, make the required settings and click Print.
