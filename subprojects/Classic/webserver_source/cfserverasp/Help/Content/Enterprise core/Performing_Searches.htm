Performing searches

All the ChemBioOffice Enterprise applications incorporate a system of forms, which allow you to search databases containing chemical and biological information. In order to search a database and retrieve results, you need to use a form and enter information within its fields. The information entered makes up a search query.
To search a database:
1. Log into the ChemBioOffice Enterprise suite of products and open an application.
2. Choose the appropriate search form.
3. Enter your search criteria and click the Search button.
