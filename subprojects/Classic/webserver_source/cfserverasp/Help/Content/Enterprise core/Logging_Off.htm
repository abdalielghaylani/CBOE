Logging off

When you access a ChemBioOffice Enterprise application, a session starts with the server. Although the server times out after a period specified by the administrator, it is recommended that you log out when your session is finished.
To log off from a ChemBioOffice Enterprise application, click Log Off in the menu bar of the application. When you log off, the server resources being used are cleared.
