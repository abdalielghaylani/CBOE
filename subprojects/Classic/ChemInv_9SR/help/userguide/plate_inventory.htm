Plate Inventory

Plates are held in locations, much like containers. For this reason, plates are browsed in the same way that any other container is browsed.  

Plates are indicated as such in the Container List Frame with a plate icon. When a plate is opened, the Plate Contents Frame is displayed in the bottom right frame.

Plates and Containers can exist in the same location.
