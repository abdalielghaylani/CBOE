Deleting General Reports

To delete a general report:

  1. Click Tasks in the menu bar of the Inventory Enterprise home page to display the Inventory Enterprise Administrative Menu page.
  2. Click Manage Reports in the Inventory Management section.
  3. Select a report type and a report layout.
  4. Click the Delete link. The following window appears:

  1. Click the OK button to delete the report .
