Creating General Reports

To create a general report:

  1. Click Tasks in the menu bar of the Inventory Enterprise home page. The Inventory Enterprise Administrative Menu page appears:

  1. Click Manage Reports in the Inventory Management section. The following window appears:

  1. Select a report type and a report layout.
  2. Click the New link. The following window appears:

  1. Enter relevant information in the various fields and click the Next button. The following window appears:

  1. Click the OK button.
  2. Click the OK button when a success message is displayed.
