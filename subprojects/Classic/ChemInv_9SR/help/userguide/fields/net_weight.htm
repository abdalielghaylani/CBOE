Net Weight

Type of field: Text

This field represents the value obtained after reducing the tare weight of a container from the initial gross weight of the container and its content. The unit of this field is always a mass.

Note: The type of the Net Weight field is, text because an older version of the software may have had the unit of mass included in the field rather than as separate amount (number) and unit (text) fields. Keeping the field as a text data type would avoid trouble for somebody with an old version of the ChemOffice Software.
