Formgroup

Each form created in BioSAR Enterprise is actually a combination of three forms: search form, list view form, and details view form. The three forms are interlinked. A formgroup refers to the group of three forms.

When creating formgroups, you are able to assign access rights to other users. If users, other than the you is given access to a formgroup, the formgroup is considered public.
