Deleting a Form

To delete an existing form:
1. Click the Manage your forms link within the BioSAR Enterprise section in the ChemOffice Enterprise home page to display the Form Management page.
2. Select the form to be deleted from the drop down list in the Form Management page.
3. Click the Delete link. A warning message appears.
4. Click the OK button to delete the form.
