Saving a form locally

All forms that you save on your local drive are private. To make a form available to others, save it on the network as a public form.
To save a form on a local drive:
1. Select the form tab.
2. Go to File>Save Local.
3. In the Save As window, select a location and enter a name for the form.
4. Click Save.

Saving a form on a network

Forms that you save on the network can be either public or private.
To save a form on a network:
1. Select the form tab.
2. Go to File>Save as.
3. In the Save Form As window:
a. Enter a name for the form.
b. In the Comments text box, enter information about the form that you want to include.
c. Select whether you want the form to be public or private.
d. Click OK.

Managing Forms

Making a public form private

To make a public form private:
1. Open the public form.
2. Save the form as a private form.
Note: After you change the status from public to private and save the form. The public form still exists. To remove the public form, delete it from the Public Forms list.
