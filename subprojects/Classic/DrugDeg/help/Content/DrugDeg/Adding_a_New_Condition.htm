Adding a New Condition

To add a new condition to the list:
1. In the CambridgeSoft Drug Degradation Enterprise page, click Manage Experimental Conditions List. The Manage Degradation Condition List appears.
2. Enter the name of a degradation condition in the textbox.
3. Click Add Record.
4. Click Main Menu to return to the CambridgeSoft Drug Degradation Enterprise page.
