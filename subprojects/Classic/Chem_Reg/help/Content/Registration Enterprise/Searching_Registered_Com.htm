Searching Registered Compounds

Registration Enterprise facilitates two types of searches: Compound search and Batch search. The difference between the two searches can be observed in the search results. The result of a Batch search displays all the compounds matching the search criteria along with their batch information whereas the result of a Compound search displays only compound information.
It also allows you to search for substances on the basis of any field. If the Registration Enterprise database contains a large number of records, make your search criteria as specific as possible. This reduces the size of the search result list and allows you to locate the required record easily and quickly. However, try broadening your search criteria if you are not able to locate the required record due to your search criteria.
NOTE:   After searching the records, you can mark the records for later review. For information about marking records, please see Marking Records.
