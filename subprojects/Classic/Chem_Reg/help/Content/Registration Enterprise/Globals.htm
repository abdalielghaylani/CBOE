Globals

ADJUST_NETSCAPE_WIDTHS
Example: ADJUST_NETSCAPE_WIDTHS = 1
Description: Corrects for the differences in widths in Netscape compared to IE. 1 will scale Netscape pages, 0 will not make any changes.
Default: 1
Options: 0 | 1
