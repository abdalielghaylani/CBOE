Administration

Administration tasks can be performed within Registration Enterprise to manage and maintain Registration Enterprise and its users. Only users having appropriate privileges can perform the administration tasks in Registration Enterprise. Various administration tasks that can be performed in Registration Enterprise are:
• Managing tables
• Defining new experiments
• Managing users and roles
• Managing analytics tables
• Changing passwords
• Importing SDFiles
• Managing workgroups
• Exporting Registration Enterprise Records to SDFiles
