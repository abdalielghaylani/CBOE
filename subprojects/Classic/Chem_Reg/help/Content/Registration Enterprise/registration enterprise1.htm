Registration Enterprise 10.0

About this Guide

Welcome to the Registration Enterprise User Guide. Inside this guide, you will find a full description of Registration Enterprise, its features, and complete instructions on how to use them. This guide is available in PDF, CHM, and Web-based formats.
• For the PDF format, refer to the documentation libarary on the CD.
• For the CHM format, go to Start>Programs>ChemOffice Enterprise 2008>Registration Enterprise10>Registration Enterprise 10.0 User’s Guide.
• For the Web-based format, launch Registration Enterprise and select Help.
