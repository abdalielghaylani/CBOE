Defining New Experiments

You can store information about the different tests performed on the compounds by defining experiments in Registration Enterprise.
To define a new experiment:
1. Click New Experiment in the Administration section in the Registration Enterprise home page. The following page containing all the experiments defined in Registration Enterprise appears:

 
2. Click new. The following page appears:

 
3. Enter the required information and click Add Record.
To edit the information in an experiment, see “Editing an Experiment” in "Experiment Type".
