Benefits
• Uses Oracle’s robust data model to manage chemical products and their properties.
• Checks for uniqueness during registration and will register duplicates as batches of existing substance.
• User administration and data entry are done through simple, easy-to-learn web forms.
• Provides an additional qualifying step before registering the compound.
• Data entry errors, or other human errors can be detected before registering the compound in your company-wide database.
• Highly customizable and easy to use.
