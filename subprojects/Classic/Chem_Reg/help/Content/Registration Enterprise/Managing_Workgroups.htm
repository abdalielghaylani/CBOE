Managing Workgroups

Workgroups refer to the users of the ChemOffice applications. The People table stores the information about the users of the ChemOffice applications. In order to manage workgroups in Registration Enterprise, click the Manage Workgroups link within the Administration section in the Registration Enterprise home page.
