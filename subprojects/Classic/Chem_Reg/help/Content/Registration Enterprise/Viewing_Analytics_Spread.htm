Viewing Analytics Spreadsheet

In the analytics spreadsheet, you can view the records grouped on the basis of the experiment type.
To view the analytics spreadsheet:
1. Click Show Spreadsheet in the Query and Reporting section in the Registration Enterprise home page. The following page appears:

 
2. Select the required experiment type from the Select Experiment Type list box.
3. Click Show S/Sheet. The spreadsheet appears:
