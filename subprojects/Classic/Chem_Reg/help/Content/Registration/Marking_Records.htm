Marking Records

You can mark the records in both list view and form view. Marked hits are user specific and can be retrieved at any time using Show Marked option under Marked menu. Marking records lets you view the records without creating a search query for them again. However, keep in mind that, as the database is updated, your list of marked records isn't automatically revised. As a result, you may need to periodically rerun your search to ensure that your list of marked records remains accurate.

To mark a record, select the check box to the extreme left of the corresponding record in list view. After marking the record, you can view or unmark the record or export the hits to other linked applications like Inventory System.

To mark all the records displayed in list view, click Mark All.

To unmark all marked records displayed, click UnMark All.
