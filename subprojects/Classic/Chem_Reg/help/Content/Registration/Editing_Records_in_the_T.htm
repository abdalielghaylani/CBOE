Editing Records in the Temporary Table

You can edit the records in the temporary table before they are registered in the permanent table.

NOTE:Editing cannot be undone.

To edit a record in the temporary table:

1. Search the temporary table for the record to edit.
2. In the results list, click Review record corresponding to the record. The Temporary Records page appears.
3. Click Edit.
4. Make the required changes in the registry, component, or batch information and click Submit to save the changes or click Cancel to undo any unsaved changes.
