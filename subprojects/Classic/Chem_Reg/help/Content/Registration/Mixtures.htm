Mixtures

A mixture is a substance which consists of two or more pure substances mixed together but not chemically combined with each other in a definite composition. The molecules of two or more different substances are mixed in the form of solutions, suspensions, and colloids. The physical properties of a mixture, such as its melting point, may differ from those of its components. Mixtures can usually be separated into its original components by mechanical means.
