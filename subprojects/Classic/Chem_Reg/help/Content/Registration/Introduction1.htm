Introduction

Welcome to Registration Enterprise. Registration lets you register compounds and mixtures that your organization acquires or synthesizes in chemical laboratories to a common database. You can add, search, and edit the compounds and batches and share registration records over a network through a simple and user-friendly graphical interface. Any user can immediately track the records you submit, enabling users to gather information stored in the database easily and quickly.

Users can enter data and administrators can manage user accounts using simple Web forms. Registration system detects any data entry errors before registering the compound in the database.
