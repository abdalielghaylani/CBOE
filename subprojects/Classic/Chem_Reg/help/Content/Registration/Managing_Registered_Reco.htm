Managing Registered Records

When you register a record to the permanent table, it is registered as a batch. You can search, edit and add batches to the registered records. Once a batch is added, you can edit, move or delete the batches added.

Registration lets you search for records on the basis of any database field. Make your search criteria as specific as possible to minimize the list of search results. Alternatively, try broadening your search criteria to locate the required record due to your search criteria.

TIP:After searching, mark the search results for later review. See Marking Records for information.
