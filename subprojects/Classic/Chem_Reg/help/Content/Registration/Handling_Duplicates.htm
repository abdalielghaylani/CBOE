Handling Duplicates

Registration Enterprise 11.0 performs an internal search and intimates you when you try to register a duplicate record. Registration lets you manage the duplicates by providing the following options:

• Duplicate- register duplicates as a new compound in Registration Enterprise.
• Temporary-lets you register all other records other than the duplicates and leaves duplicates in temporary table.
• Batch- lets you add new batches for the duplicate compounds.
• None- leave the record in temporary table.
