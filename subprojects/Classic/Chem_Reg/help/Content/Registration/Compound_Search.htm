Compound Search

To search the registered compounds in the permanent table:

1. Click Search Registry option in the Registration home page. The Search Permanent Registry page appears.

 

2. Click Retrieve All to retrieve all records in permanent table or search for a record by specifying a search criteria and click Search. The records meeting the criteria specified appears.
NOTE:For more information on search criteria, refer to ChemBioOffice Enterprise 11 guide.

To view record information, click Show Details link corresponding to the record.
