ChemACX Form Fields

Below is a list of all fields in the ChemACX forms.

Compound attributes:

  • Substance Name.
  • ACX Number: This text field holds ACX number, which is unique identifier and registry number for the substances stored in ChemACX and the substances submitted by Open Chemistry. ACX number can store the vendor name, quality, package size, and physical material of the substances.
  • MolWeight Range.
  • Substructure: This structure field holds the structure or substructure of a substance. The structure of a substance is drawn using the ChemDraw plug-in. For information about the ChemDraw plug-in, please refer to the ChemDraw Quick Reference Guide.

Batch attributes:

  • CAS Registry Number: This text field holds the CAS registry number. This field ensures uniqueness of a compound and is assigned to every uniquely-identifiable compound.
  • Molecular Formula.
  • Catalog Number.
