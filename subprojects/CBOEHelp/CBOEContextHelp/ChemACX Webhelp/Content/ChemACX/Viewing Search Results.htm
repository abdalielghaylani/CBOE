Viewing Search Results

You can browse your search results in either List View or Details View. Both views offer several navigation and records management features:

  • Click on the following icon to zoom-in on the structure of a chemical.
  • Click on the following icon to view the synonyms of a chemical.
  • Click on the following icon to view the search results in the List view.
  • Click on the following icon to mark a chemical record for later review.
  • Register a chemical in Registration Enterprise.
  • Access the MSDS data for a chemical.
  • Browse vendor information.
  • Add products to your shopping cart.
