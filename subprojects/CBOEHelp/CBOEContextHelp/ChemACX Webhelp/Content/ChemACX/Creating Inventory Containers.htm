Creating Inventory Containers

To create containers in Inventory Enterprise from ChemACX chemicals:

  1. Open the shopping cart.
  2. Click Send to Inventory Manager. The Create Inventory Containers from ChemACX page appears.

Note: Consult your system administrator, if you do not see the Send to Inventory manager button.

  1. Provide the required information and click OK. The following page appears.

  1. Click Commit. The following page appears.

  1. Click View to view the new containers.
