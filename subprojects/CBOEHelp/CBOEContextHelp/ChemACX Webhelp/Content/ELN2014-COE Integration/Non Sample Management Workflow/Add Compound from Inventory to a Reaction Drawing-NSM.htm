Adding Compounds from Inventory System to a Reaction Drawing - NSM

To add a compound from Inventory to a reaction drawing:

  1. After executing the search, select a compound from the Show Results tab of the Select & Add Structure dialog.
  2. Click Add. The following menu displays:
  • Left of Arrow as Reactant
  • Above Arrow as Reagent
  • Right of Arrow as Product
  • To Grid as Reagent
  1. Select an option from the drop down menu, for example, Right of Arrow as Product.

The chemical structure is added to the Reaction field of the Reaction W COE Integration section. A new entry in the Reactants & Products table appears in the Reaction W COE Integration section.
