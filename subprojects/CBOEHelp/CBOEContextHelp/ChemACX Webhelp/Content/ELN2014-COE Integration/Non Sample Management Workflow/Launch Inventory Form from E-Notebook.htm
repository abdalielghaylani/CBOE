Launch Inventory Form from E-Notebook

You can launch an Inventory form and view an Inventory container if a valid barcode is present. It is accessed from Reactants & Products table in the Reaction W COE Integration section.

To launch an Inventory form from E-Notebook:

  1. Ensure the barcode column contains a valid Barcode value.
  2. Press the <Ctrl> key + click on the barcode value that appears as a reference.

An Inventory form is launched and displays the container details of the selected barcode.
