Registering PS Products

Temporary Registration

The Temporary Registration method gathers information and attempts to register the compound in the Registration system. Upon successful registration, a Temp ID is returned to the Registration Table within the PS Registry Form.

  1. In the Selected column of the Registration Table, select the check boxes corresponding to the compounds you want to register.
  2. Click the Registration form tool and select Temporary Registration.

The Registration dialog appears and asks you to confirm the registration.

  1. Click Yes. Another Registration dialog appears displaying the Registration results.
  2. Click OK.

The Temp ID is returned to the Registration Table within the PS Registry Form for all selected compounds.  See the example below.

Permanent Registration

The Permanent Registration method gathers information and attempts to register the compound into the Registration system. Upon successful registration, a Reg Number and Batch Number are returned to the Registration Table within the PS Registry Form.

  1. In the Selected column of the Registration Table, select the check boxes corresponding to the compounds you want to register.
  2. Click the Registration form tool and select Permanent Registration.

The Registration dialog appears and asks you to confirm the registration.

  1. Click Yes. Another Registration dialog appears displaying the Registration Results.
  2. Click OK.

The Reg Numbers and Batch Numbers are returned to the Registration Table within the PS Registry Form for all selected compounds. See the example below.
