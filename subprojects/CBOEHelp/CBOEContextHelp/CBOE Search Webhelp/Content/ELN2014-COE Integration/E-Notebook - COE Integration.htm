E-Notebook 17.1 - COE 17.1 Integration

The E-Notebook - COE integration lets you integrate E-Notebook 17.1 with other ChemOffice Enterprise applications such as, Inventory, ChemACX, and Registry. This part of the guide explains in detail the data flow between E-Notebook and COE applications such as Inventory, Registration, and ChemACX. It also explains the bulk container creation, and bulk registration for Combi Chem Experiments.
