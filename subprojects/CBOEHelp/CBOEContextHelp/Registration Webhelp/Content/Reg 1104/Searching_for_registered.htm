Searching for registered compounds

To search the registered compounds in the permanent table:

  1. Select Search Registry option in the Registration home page.

  1. The Search Permanent Registry page appears.



  1. Do one of the following:
  • To return all records, click on Retrieve All.
  • To search for specific records, fill in the search fields to specify your search criteria and then click on Searchbutton.
