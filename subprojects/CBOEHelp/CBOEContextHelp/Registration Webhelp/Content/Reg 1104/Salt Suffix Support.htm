Salt Suffix Support

This topic covers the Salt Suffix support in Registration.

If your registration system has the following settings: EnableFragments=True and SameBatchesIdentify=False, the salt suffix feature will be supported. When a user submits a record to which a salt is associated, the salt suffix type will be included in the Full Registry Number. To apply this, please perform the steps below:

  1. Log in to ChemBioOffice Enterprise Manager, click "Main Menu" under Registration Enterprise. The Registrations' home page appears.
  2. Click Submit New Component icon in the Registration home page or in the ChemBioOffice Manager home page under Registration Enterprise. The Submit Component page appears.
  3. Fill in the fields with specific data.
  4. Select the Prefix and then add a salt in the Fragment Information table.
  5. Click Submit, to save the record in the Temporary table.
  6. If you have adequate privileges, click Approve and then Register to Register the record in the Permanent table, or wait for the Reviewer/Registrar to Approve/Register the record.
  7. After the record is registered, the newly added salt formula (In this case the Salt Suffix Type was set to Formulae, three other options may be selected: Codes, IDs or Descriptions) will be displayed in the Full Registry Number as shown in the screen shot below:
