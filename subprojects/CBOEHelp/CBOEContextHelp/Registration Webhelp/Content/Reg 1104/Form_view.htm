Form view

The form view displays all the information for a particular record. To display a record in form view:

  • Click the "Review Record" link corresponding to a specific record.
  • The corresponding record's fields will be displayed. The user will be able to navigate through the Registry, Component and Batch information via the tree exiting in the left frame as shown in the screen shot below:
