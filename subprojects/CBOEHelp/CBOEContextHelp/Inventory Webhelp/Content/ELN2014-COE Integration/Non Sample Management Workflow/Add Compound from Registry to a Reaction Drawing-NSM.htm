Add Compound from Registry System to a Reaction Drawing - NSM

To add components from Registry to a reaction drawing:

  1. From the Select & Add Structure dialog, the Show Results tab, click Add.
  1. Select an option from the drop down list:
  • Left of Arrow as Reactant
  • Top of Arrow as Reagent
  • Right of Arrow as Product
  • To Grid as Reagent

The chemical structure is added to the Reaction field of the Reaction W COE Integration section. A new entry in the Reactants & Products table also appears in the Reaction W COE Integration section.
