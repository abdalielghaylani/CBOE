Registration Single Click

When you have adequate privileges, you can permanently register samples via a single click.

To register permanently a record via a single click:

  1. Create a sample and assign a product to it.
  2. Navigate to the Samples section and click on Permanent Registration form tool under Home ribbon. A dialog appears showing the Registry and the Batch numbers.

  1. Click Ok to close the Registration dialog.
