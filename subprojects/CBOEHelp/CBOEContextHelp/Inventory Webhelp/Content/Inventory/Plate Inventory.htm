Plate Inventory

Plates hold multiple compounds in an easily-transportable unit. In Inventory Enterprise, plates are analogous to containers. Similar to containers, plates are held in locations and are managed in the same way as containers.

Like containers, plates are managed in the Container Management area of Inventory Enterprise. However, the Container List frame and Container Details frame pertain to containers only. The Plate List frame and Plate Details frame pertain to plates only. The following example displays the Container Management area with plates listed.

The top right frame contains the Plate List. The bottom right frame contains the Plate Details. For more information about the links and tabs in the Plate List and Plate Details frames, see Viewing Contents of a Plate.
