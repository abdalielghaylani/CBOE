Container Management

Inventory Enterprise helps chemical and pharmaceutical research centers manage the containers associated with the reagents procured, or produced, by the research centers. The container management tasks are performed in the Container Management area of Inventory Enterprise. Users can access the Container Management area by clicking the Browse link within the Inventory Enterprise section in the ChemBioOffice Enterprise home page.

The Container Management area consists of four main frames, which are:

  • Location Tree (Left frame)
  • Current Location (Top frame)
  • Container List (Top right)
  • Container Details (Bottom right)

The various tasks that can be performed in the Container Management area, are:

  • Manage locations
  • Create a new container
  • Search containers
  • View container contents
