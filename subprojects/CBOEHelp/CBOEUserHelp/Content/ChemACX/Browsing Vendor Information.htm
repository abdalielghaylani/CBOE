Browsing Vendor Information

To Browse the vendor information in the Details view:

  1. Click on one of the following tabs:
  • All Vendors: Lists all the vendors in ChemACX.
  • Your Favorites: Lists only the favorites vendors.
  1. Select the vendor from the list. The catalogs associated with the selected vendor appear next to the vendors list, as shown below:

  1. Hold down the CTRL key and select multiple vendors to compare catalogs.
  2. Click Catalog Details corresponding to a catalog. A description of the chemical from the vendor's catalog appears:

  1. Click Close.
  2. Click Compact View to view the catalogs in the Compact view, as shown below:

By default, the catalogs appear in the Catalog view. To return to the Catalog view, click the Catalog view tab.

In the Details view, you can view the shopping cart and add products to it. Please refer to "Using the shopping cart" topic.
