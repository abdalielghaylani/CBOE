ChemACX Introduction

Welcome to the ChemACX Chapter.

This chapter provides a full description of ChemACX, its features, and complete instructions on how to use them.

ChemACX (Available Chemicals Exchange) is a read-only database of chemical products that are currently available from chemical manufacturers and distributors. The database offers complete catalogs of major world suppliers of fine research, speciality, and industrial chemicals.
