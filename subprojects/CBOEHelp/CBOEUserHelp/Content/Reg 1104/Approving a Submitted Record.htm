Approving a Submitted Record

After a submitter submits a component, the record is stored in the temporary database waiting for the approval.

The reviewer has adequate privileges to approve the submitted records. Without approving a record the Registrar will not be able to register a record and store it in the permanent database.

To approve a submitted record:

  1. Log in to CBOE with reviewer privileges
  2. Click on Review Register icon under Registration to display the approved and unapproved records.
  3. To approve multiple records at once, check the Marked checkbox corresponding to the records that you want to approve and then click on Approve Marked link

  1. To approve records one by one, click on Review Record link corresponding to each record and then click on the thumb to approve.
  2. Alternatively, click on Search Temp icon under Registration, the search form displays. Click on Retrieve All button to display all the records stored in the temporary table or enter specific criteria and click on Search button to retrieve records that meet specific criteria

  1. The records matching the query display:

  1. Click on the Review Record link corresponding to a specific record and then click on Edit button to edit the record's data when necessary

  1. Once review and edit are completed, click on the Not Approved Thumb to approve the record
  2. The record is now approved and ready to be registered.
