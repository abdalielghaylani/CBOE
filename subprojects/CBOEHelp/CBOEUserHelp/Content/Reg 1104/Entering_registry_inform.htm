Entering Registry Information

 
The Registry Information section consists of the following fields:
 
  • Identifiers:The identifiers describe chemical substances in terms of layers of information to facilitate the search for such information (Ex. Alias).
  • Registry Comments: Any comments about the substance.
 
The administrator defines the Identifiers and the Registry Number Format. For more details please contact the administrator.
  1. Fill in the Registry Information by selecting the Identifiers values from their corresponding dropdown list.
  • If the registry record is assigned to more than one identifier, click Add Identifier and select the required identifier type.

To remove an identifier, highlight the identifier's row and then click on Remove Identifier.

  1. The Registry Comments is a free text field, any comment may be entered and associated with the record.
