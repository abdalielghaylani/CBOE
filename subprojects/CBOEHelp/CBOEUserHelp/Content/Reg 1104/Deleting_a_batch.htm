Deleting a Batch

If a batch is not anymore required, it may be possible to delete it.

To delete a batch:

  1. Click the "Reg Number" link corresponding to the record. The Registry Record page appears.
  2. Highlight the batch to be deleted, and then click on Delete Batch button

  1. The following page appears. Click Delete Batch button.
