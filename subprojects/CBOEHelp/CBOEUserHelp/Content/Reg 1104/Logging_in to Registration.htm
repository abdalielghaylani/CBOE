Logging in to Registration

To open the Registration Enterprise home page:

  1. Enter http://servername/COEManager into your web browser address bar. <servername> is the name of the server where ChemBioOffice Enterprise is installed. The ChemBioOffice Enterprise's log in page appears.
  2. Enter your username and password in the ChemBioOffice Enterprise login page and click "Log in" button
  3. In the ChemBioOffice Enterprise Manager home page, click "Main Menu" under Registration Enterprise. The Home Page of Registration appears.
