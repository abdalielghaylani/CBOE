Searching for Records in the Temporary Table

It is possible to retrieve all the records in the temporary table or only specific records. Searches by structure, temporary ID, or by any other parameter may be run.

To search in the temporary table for a record:

  1. Go To "Search Temp" option in the Registration Enterprise home page or click on "Search Temp" icon under Registration System section

  1. The Search Temporary Records page displays:

  1. Do one of the following:
  • To retrieve all records in the temporary table, click Retrieve All.
  • To retrieve records that meet specific criteria, enter the search criteria under Temporary Information and then click Search. A list of records matching the search criteria you specified appears.

  • To select all of the records displayed, check the Marked checkbox. The count of the marked hits appears on the right side of the page.
  • To deselect all the selected records, uncheck the Marked checkbox. For more information on marking records, please refer to "Marking Records" topic.
