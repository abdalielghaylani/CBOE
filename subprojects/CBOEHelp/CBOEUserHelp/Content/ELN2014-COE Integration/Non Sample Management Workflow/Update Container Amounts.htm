Update Container Amounts

Update Container Amounts functionality lets you reduce the consumed amount/volume of a substance, usually these fields are: sample mass and volume. This is applied in the Reaction W COE Integration section > Reactants & Products table where inventory reagents are used, and where you can perform a bulk update to an inventory container--of the amount consumed for multiple containers.

The amount/volume specified in E-Notebook is the consumed amount and, therefore, is reduced from the inventory container.

Example:

  • Container Name:Test 1, Container ID: C1141

  • Container Name: Test 2, Container ID: C1142

The amount/volume consumed by the container entries present in E-Notebook are reduced from the container entries in the Inventory system.

To update Container Amounts:

  1. Navigate to the Reactants table in the Reaction W COE Integration section.

Note: The container in the Reactants & Products table must have Barcode values.

  1. Ensure the Updated (check box) is unchecked.
  2. Modify the Sample Mass and Vol (volume) values.

Note:  You can only reduce the consumed amount.

  1. Fill in the consumed volume.
  2. Click the Send to Inventory form tool and select Update Container Amounts.

The Update Container Amounts dialog appears for confirmation.

  1. Click Yes to confirm your action.

The Update Container Amount dialog with the created barcodes displays.

  1. Click OK.

Once the container amounts are updated in E-Notebook, the amount/volume consumed by the containers in E-Notebook is deducted form the amount/volume of the containers in the Inventory system.
