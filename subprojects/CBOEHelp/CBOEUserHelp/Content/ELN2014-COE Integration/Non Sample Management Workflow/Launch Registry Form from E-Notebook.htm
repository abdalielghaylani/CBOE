Launch Registry Form from E-Notebook

The Launch Registry Form lets you view the component details in the Registry system if a valid registration number is present.The Launch Registry Form is accessed from Reactants & Products table within the Reaction W COE Integration section.

  1. Ensure the Reg Number column has a valid registration number value.
  2. Press <Ctrl> key and click on the Reg Number that appears as a reference.

A registry form appears and displays the registry information of the selected reg number.
