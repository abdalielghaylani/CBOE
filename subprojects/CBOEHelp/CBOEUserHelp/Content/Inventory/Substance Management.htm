Substance Management

The Inventory compounds can be managed via the Substance Management feature in Inventory Enterprise. You can launch the substance management page by clicking the Manage Substances link within the Inventory Enterprise section of the ChemBioOffice Enterprise home page, or by clicking the Manage Substances link in the menu bar of the Inventory Enterprise home page. The following is an example of the Substance Management area.

You can perform the following tasks from the Substance Management page:

  • Creating a new substance
  • Adding the Health and Safety Data
  • Searching substances
