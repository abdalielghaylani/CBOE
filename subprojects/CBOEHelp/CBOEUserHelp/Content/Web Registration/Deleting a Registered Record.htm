Deleting a Registered Record

If you have adequate privileges, you can delete a registered record.

To delete a record, do one of the following:

  • In the Registration Records page, click on the Detail View icon corresponding to the record that you want to delete. The records details view displays. Click Delete button.

  • In the Registration Records page, mark the record(s) that you want to delete and click Delete Marked.
