Manage Group Section

The Manage Group section is accessible by any user who has the "CSS_MANAGE_GROUPS" privilege assigned.

In order to access the Manage Group Section:

  1. Log in the ChemOfficeEnterprise with CSS_MANAGE_GROUPS privilege
  2. Under Security section click on "Manage Groups" icon

  1. The following application opens:

  1. There are three sections:
  • The left section shows groups in hierarchical order
  • The upper right side section shows the Group Users
  • The lower right section shows the Group User Details

  • Moving a Group
  • Editing a Group
  • Removing a Group
