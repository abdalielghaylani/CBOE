Adding a Group

To add a group:

  1. Highlight a Parent group and click on the Add group icon in the toolbar

  1. This will open a popup for the group details:
  • Group Name: Enter the name of the group
  • Leader: Select a Group Leader
  • Roles: These will be default roles for all members of the group. The user of any group will have the group granted roles in addition to the default roles they have

  1. Enter the required information and click on the Save button. The Group is added in the tree as shown in the screen shot below
