Logging In to DocManager

To use DocManager, you must first log into ChemBioOffice Enterprise.

To log in:

  1. Enter http://<servername>/coemanager into your web browser where <servername> is the name of your webserver. The ChemBioOffice Webserver Global Login page appears.
  2. At the login screen, enter your username and password and click log in. The ChemBioOffice Enterprise main Web page appears
