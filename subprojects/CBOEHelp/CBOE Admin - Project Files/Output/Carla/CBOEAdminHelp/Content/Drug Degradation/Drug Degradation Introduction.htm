Drug Degradation Introduction

Drug Degradation lets you manage records for reagents that degrade over time. You can keep track of products, reactions, and the environments in which the degradation reactions take place. Using the ChemBioOffice Webserver, you can also look up records from other Drug Degradation users.

This chapter provides a full description of Drug Degradation, its features, and complete instructions on how to use them.
