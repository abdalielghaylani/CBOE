Viewing Point Data

When you position the mouse over a point, the status bar shows the X and Y values of the point. For a standard plot, the record number is also shown.

The record number, X, and Y values for the highlighted record.

Click a point on a standard plot, and the form will move to the corresponding record in the hitlist.
