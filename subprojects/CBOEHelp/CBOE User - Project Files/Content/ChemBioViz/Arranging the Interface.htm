Arranging the Interface

You can move the Record Toolbar, navigation pane, form editing Properties panes, and similar interface objects to where ever you need them. To move an object, click+drag it to the new location. To move a menu or pane, click+drag its title bar.

Click a title bar (circled above) to turn the menu into a floating window.
