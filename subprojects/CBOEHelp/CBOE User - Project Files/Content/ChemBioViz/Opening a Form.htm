Opening a Form

To open a form stored on a network drive, select either the Public Forms or My Forms button in the navigation pane. Then, select the form you want.

To open a form stored on a local drive, go to File>Open Local and select the form.

After you open a form, the form name and its type (User or Public) appears in the title bar.

The ChemBioViz title bar displays the type and name of the form that is currently open.
