Duplicating a Form

To duplicate an existing form:

  1. Open any form.

Tip: To save time, consider starting with a form that already resembles the one you want to create.

  1. Right-click the Form tab and go to Add Tab>Duplicate Form. A new tab appears, displaying a duplicate of the form tab you selected.

  1. (Optional) To change the name of the tab, right-click the tab and select Rename Tab.
