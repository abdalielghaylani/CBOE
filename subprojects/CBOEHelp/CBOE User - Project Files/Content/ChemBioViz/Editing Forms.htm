Editing Forms

You have numerous options for editing a form. For example, you can add or remove form elements, change their appearances, and select which data they display.

Note: Just administrator users are allowed to edit forms.

To open a form for editing:

  1. Open the form. If it is already open, select its tab.
  2. In the navigation pane, click Edit Form Tab.

After you open a form for editing, you can modify it using form elements.
