Managing Forms

To make a public form private:

  1. Open the public form.
  2. Go to File>Save as.
  3. In the Save Form As window, select private and click OK.

Note: After you change the status from public to private and save the form. The public form still exists. To remove the public form, delete it from the Public Forms list.

To make a private form public:

  1. Open the private form.
  2. Go to File>Save as.
  3. In the Save Form As window, select public and click OK.

Note: After you change the status from private to public and save the form. The private form still exists. To remove the private form, delete it from the My Forms list.

To close a form:

  1. Select the form tab.
  2. Go to File>Close.

Delete a form:

To delete a form, right-click the form in either the Public Forms or My Forms list and click Delete Tab.
