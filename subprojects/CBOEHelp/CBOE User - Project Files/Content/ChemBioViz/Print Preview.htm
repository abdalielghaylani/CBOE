Print Preview

To preview the form or table before printing it:

  1. Go to File>Print Preview.
  2. When particular rows in the table are selected for printing the following dialog displays:

  1. Click on Yes button to preview only the selected records

  1. Click on Nobutton to preview all records

  1. Users can then browse through the pages to see what they will look like when they are printed.
