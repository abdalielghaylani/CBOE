Substance Management

The Inventory substances are managed in the Substance Management area of Inventory Enterprise. The Substance Management area is accessed by clicking the Manage Substances link within the Inventory Enterprise section of the ChemBioOffice Enterprise home page, or by clicking the Manage Substances link in the menu bar of the Inventory Enterprise home page. The following is an example of the Substance Management area.

You can perform the following tasks from the Substance Management area:

  • Creating a new substance
  • Adding the Health and Safety Data
  • Searching substances
