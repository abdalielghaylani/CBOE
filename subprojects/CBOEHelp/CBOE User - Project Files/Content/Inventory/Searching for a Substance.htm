Searching for a Substance

You can search for substances in Inventory Enterprise using the search form displayed in the Substance Management area of Inventory Enterprise.

The Substance Management area is accessed by clicking the Manage Substances link in the Inventory Enterprise section of the ChemBioOffice Enterprise home page.

The search form in Substance Management is a simplified version of the Substructure Search tab window. It lets you search Inventory Enterprise on the basis of the Substructure, Substance Name, CAS Registry #, ACX Number, Molecular Formula, MolWeight Range, Compound ID, and Compound Type fields.

Note: You can also access a substance search form through the Substance tab window of the Create or Edit an Inventory Container window, which displays when you click the New Container link in the Current Location frame. This search form is used when you need to search a substance to add to a container. For more information about searching a substance through the Substance tab window, see "Substance Tab" under Creating a New Container.

To search for a substance in Inventory Enterprise from the Substance Management area:

  1. From the ChemBioOffice Enterprise home page, Inventory Enterprise section, click the Manage Substances link.
  2. Enter the search criteria for your query.
  3. Click Search. The search results display in list format, listing all substances matching the search criteria you specified in the search form. For detailed information about a particular substance, click the Details tab corresponding to the substance.

After searching for a substance in Inventory Enterprise, you can perform the following tasks:

  • Edit the substance
  • Delete the substance
  • View history
  • Manage synonyms
  • Manage links

Editing a Substance

To edit an Inventory Enterprise substance:

  1. Perform a search to get a list of results (as covered in the previous section, "To search for a substance in Inventory Enterprise....")  The following is an example of search results.  In the upper-right corner of the window is information about the number of substances that match your query, called, "Substances matching your query: number, for example, 435".

  1. Click the Details tab corresponding to the appropriate substance. A window containing the details, such as molecular weight and molecular formula of the substance appears.

  1. Click the Edit Mode button. The Create or Edit a substance in Inventory Enterprise window, containing the substance details in the edit mode, appears.

Note: If the results of your search is a single compound, you can access the Edit Mode button directly, as the Details tab is not available.

  1. Make your edits and click OK. A window informing you that the substance is updated successfully in Inventory Enterprise appears.

Note: A Conflict Resolution screen appears if a unique field, such as, Substance Name and CAS Registry #, already exists for an existing Inventory substance.

  1. Click OK.

Note: If you are using Google Chrome browser, you have to save the metadata edits before you update the chemical structure, otherwise you will lose your metadata edits.

Deleting a Substance

To delete an Inventory Enterprise substance:

  1. Perform a search to get a list of results (as covered at the beginning of this topic, "To search for a substance in Inventory Enterprise....").
  2. Click the Details tab corresponding to the appropriate substance. A window containing the details, such as molecular weight and molecular formula of the substance appears.
  3. Click Delete Record. A confirmation dialog appears, "Are you sure you want to delete this substance?"

  1. Click OK to confirm the deletion. A window appears and informs you that the substance is deleted from Inventory Enterprise.
  2. Click OK.

Viewing History

Substance history in Inventory Enterprise lets you track the actions performed on a particular substance. You can view the history of a substance using audit reports. And, you can filter the actions listed in the audit report for information about specific actions only.

Inventory Enterprise provides two types of audit reports:

  • Standard audit report
  • Aggregate audit report

Managing Synonyms

To add, edit or remove a substance's synonyms do the following:

  1. Perform a search to get a list of results (as covered at the beginning of this topic, "To search for a substance in Inventory Enterprise....").
  2. Click the Details tab corresponding to the appropriate substance. A window containing the details, such as molecular weight and molecular formula of the substance appears.

  1. Click the Manage Synonyms link. The Manage Substance Synonyms window appears.

In the Manage Substance Synonyms window, you can create, edit, or delete synonyms.

Creating a New Synonym

To create a new synonym:

  1. Click the New link in the Manage Substance Synonyms window. The Create a New Synonym window appears.
  2. Enter the synonym in the Synonym text field.
  3. Click OK. The newly-created synonym is added to the list of synonyms displayed in the Manage Substance Synonyms window.

Editing a Synonym

To edit a synonym:

  1. From the Manage Substance Synonyms window, click Edit next to the synonym you want to edit. The Create a New Synonym dialog, containing the synonym in the edit mode, appears.
  2. Edit the synonym's name in the Synonym text field.
  3. Click OK.

Deleting a Synonym

To delete a synonym:

  1. From the Manage Substance Synonyms window, click Delete next to the synonym you want to delete. The Create a New Synonym window appears and confirms the deletion operation.
  2. Click OK.

Managing Links

Inventory Enterprise lets you associate links with a substance. Clicking on these links gathers additional information about the substance.

To access the Manage Links window:

  1. Perform a search to get a list of results (as covered at the beginning of this topic, "To search for a substance in Inventory Enterprise....").
  2. Click the Details tab corresponding to the appropriate substance. A window containing the details, such as molecular weight and molecular formula of the substance appears.
  3. Click the Manage Links link.

  1. The Manage Links window appears.

In the Manage Links window, you can create, edit, or delete links:

Creating a New Link

To create a new link:

  1. From the Manage Links window, click the New link. The Create/Edit/Delete an Inventory Link window appears.
  2. Enter the URL for this link in the URL text field.
  3. Enter link text in the Link Text text field.
  4. Click OK. You can access the link from the Substance tab in the Container Details frame.

Editing a Link

To edit a link:

  1. From the Manage Links window, click the Edit link next to the link you want to edit. The Create/Edit/Delete an Inventory Link window, containing the link information in the edit mode, appears.
  2. Make your edits to this link.
  3. Click OK.

Deleting a Link

To delete a link:

  1. From the Manage Links window, Click the Delete link next to the link you want to delete. The Create/Edit/Delete an Inventory Link window appears, where you confirm the deletion operation.
  2. Click OK to delete the link.
