Registering MPS Products

RegTemp

The RegTemp Method gathers up the information and attempts to register the compound into the registration system. Upon successful registration, a TempID will be returned to the Registration Table of the MPS Registry Form.

  1. Select the “Selected” checkbox for each of the compounds you want to register

  1. Click “Registration with COE11” form tool and choose “Reg Temp” option
  2. The Registration dialog appears for confirmation

  1. Click Yes for the “Registration” dialog. “Registration” dialog appears displaying the “Registration results”

  1. Click OK for the “Registration” dialog. TempIDs will be returned to the Registration Table of the MPS Registry Form for all the selected compounds

RegPerm

The RegPerm Method gathers up the information and attempts to register the Compound into the registration system. Upon successful registration, a Reg Number and Batch Number will be returned to the Registration Table of the MPS Registry Form.

  1. Select the “Selected” checkbox for each of the compounds you want to register

  1. Click “Registration with COE11” form tool and choose “Reg Perm” option
  2. Registration dialog appears for confirmation

  1. Click Yes for the “Registration” dialog. “Registration” dialog appears displaying the “Registration results”

  1. Click Ok, the Reg Numbers and Batch Numbers will be returned to the Registration Table of the MPS Registry Form for all the selected compounds
