Reg Perm

The Reg Perm gathers up the information and attempts to register the samples in bulk into the registration system. Upon successful registration, a Reg Number and Batch Number will be returned to the Samples Table of the Reaction and Samples Properties of the Sample Information section.

  1. After creating samples and assigning products to it, select the samples to register, click on the "Sample Registration" form tool and select "Reg Temp" option
  2. Registration dialog appears for confirmation

  1. Click on Yes button. Registration dialog displays with the Registration results

  1. Click on Ok button. Reg Numbers and Batch Numbers will be returned to the Samples Table under the Reaction section

  1. Go to the Samples section, the same Reg Numbers and Batch Numbers will be displayed under Sample Information SM>>Properties table corresponding to the selected samples
