Add Compound from Registry system to a reaction drawing

From the Show results tab,

  1. Click the Add button, the following menu items displays:
  • Left of Arrow as Reactant
  • Top of Arrow as Reagent
  • Right of Arrow as Product
  • To Grid as Reagent
  1. Select one of the option above

  1. The Chemical Structure from the Add-in added to the Reaction field of the "Reaction" section accordingly. A new entry in the Reactants or products table also appears in the "Reaction" section.
