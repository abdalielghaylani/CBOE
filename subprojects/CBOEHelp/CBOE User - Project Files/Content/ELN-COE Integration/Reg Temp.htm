Reg Temp

The Reg Temp gathers up the information and attempts to register the samples in bulk into the registration system. Upon successful registration, a Temp ID will be returned to the samples table of the Reaction section and Samples Properties of the Sample Information section.

  1. After creating samples and assigning products to it, click on the "Sample Registration" form tool and select "Reg Temp" option
  2. Registration dialog appears for confirmation

  1. Click on Yes button. Registration dialog displays with the Registration results

  1. Click on Ok button. Temp IDs will be returned to the Samples Table under the Reaction section

  1. Go to the Samples section, the same Temp IDs will be displayed under Sample Information SM>>Properties table corresponding to the selected samples.
