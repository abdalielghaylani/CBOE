Parallel Synthesis Bulk Registration and Container Creation

Bulk registration and bulk container creation are configured for Combi Chem Experiments. Products from combinatorial synthesis are used for bulk registration and bulk container creation.

To create a bulk container:

  1. Create and enumerate a Parallel Synthesis (PS)experiment.
  2. After enumerating the PS Experiment, navigate to the PS Summary section. Note that the enumerated products/reactants and associated data from combinatorial synthesis are available in the PS Summary section after the enumerated PS experiment is saved in the E-Notebook.
  3. Click the check box in the Selected column to select the products to send to the PS Registry Form section.
  4. Click the Copy to Registry Form form tool.

The Export Selection To Collection confirmation dialog appears.

  1. Click Yes.

The selected products are copied to the Registration Table of the PS Registry Form section.

  1. In the Registration Properties table, select the Project, Scientist and Prefix from the drop down list and enter a Synthesis Date.
