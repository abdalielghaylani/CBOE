Quick Temporary Registration

Quick Temporary Registration gathers information and attempts to register the samples in bulk into the Registration System. Upon successful registration, a Temp ID is returned to the Samples table in the Reaction/Process Reaction section and to the Properties table in the Sample Information section.

To register a sample temporarily:

  1. Create sample(s) and assign products to it.
  2. Within the Samples table > Selected column, check the samples you want to register.
  3. Click on the Registration form tool and select Quick Temporary Registration. A Registration dialog appears for confirmation of the registration.

  1. Click Yes. The Registration dialog displays with the registration results, for example:

  1. Click OK. The Temp ID is returned to the Samples table in the Reaction section.

  1. Navigate to the Samples section, the same Temp ID displays under Sample Information SM>>Properties table and corresponds to the selected sample.

Note: The samples that are registered temporarily are summarized in the Open Registrations section.
