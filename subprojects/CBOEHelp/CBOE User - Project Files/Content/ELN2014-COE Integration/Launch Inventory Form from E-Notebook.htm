Launch Inventory Form from E-Notebook

You can view the contents of an Inventory container if a valid barcode is available.

Note: Ensure the barcode column contains a valid barcode prior to launching the Inventory form.

To launch the Inventory form:

  1. Press the <Ctrl> key and click the barcode value that appears as a reference in the Barcode column, as shown below:

An Inventory form launches and displays the container details of the selected barcode, as shown below:

Note:  Entering a valid barcode in the Reactants & Products table does not automatically retrieve the container details from the Inventory system. It is not supported in the Reaction section, it is only supported in the Materials and Prep section.

The following example of the Reactants & Products table shows a valid barcode entered in the second row, Barcode: 333, with no container details fetched from the Inventory system.

However, you can still launch the corresponding container details in the Inventory system by pressing the <Ctrl> key + clicking on the barcode value in the Reactants & Products table.
