Sending Registration Data - Non Sample Management (NSM)

You can register compounds in Registration Enterprise through E-Notebook application.

To register compounds from the E-Notebook application:

  1. Log in to E-Notebook.
  2. Create a notebook and add an experiment.
  3. Fill in the Title section and add a Reaction W COE Integration section to your experiment.
  4. Draw a reaction in the Reaction field and save.
