ChemACX Integration

You can register the ChemACX chemicals in Registration Enterprise or send the ChemACX chemicals to Inventory Enterprise, if Registration Enterprise and Inventory Enterprise are installed and ChemACX is configured appropriately.

Note: For help integrating ChemACX with either Registration Enterprise or Inventory Enterprise, see your system administrator.
