Non-Plugin Searches

A non-plugin search limits the search fields to only chemical name and CAS number. This search method is useful when you do not need to search ChemACX by substructure, and therefore you do not need the Chem and Bio Draw plugin.

To perform a non-plugin search in ChemACX:

  1. Log in to ChemACX and click on on the Search button to display the search form.
  2. Click on the "Click for Non-Plugin Search" link.
    The following page appears:

  1. Enter the name or CAS number of the chemical that you are searching for.
  2. Click on Search button.
