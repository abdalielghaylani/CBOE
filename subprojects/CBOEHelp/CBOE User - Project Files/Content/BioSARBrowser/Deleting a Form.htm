Deleting a Form

To delete an existing form:

  1. From the ChemOffice Enterprise home page, BioSAR Enterprise section, click the Manage your forms. The Form Management interface appears.
  2. Select the form you want to delete from the drop-down list.
  3. Click Delete. A warning message appears.
  4. Click OK to delete the form.
