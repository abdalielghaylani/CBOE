Form Management

BioSAR Enterprise lets you search and view data in multiple application databases simultaneously. To search and view data, you define the search criteria and return values. The Form Management page lets you create and customize the search criteria and return values.

Note: You must have the appropriate privileges assigned to create forms.  Consult your system administrator regarding privileges to create new forms.

To open the Form Management page:

  1. Click Manage your forms in the login page of ChemOffice Enterprise, BioSAR Enterprise section.

  1. You can also click Open Form in the login page of ChemBioOffice Enterprise, BioSAR Enterprise section..

The BioSAR Enterprise forms display.

  1. Select Forms > Manage > Forms.

The Form Management page displays.
