Deleting Records from the Temporary Table

If the user has appropriate privileges, he/she can delete a record from the temporary table. The Reviewer and the Registrar are allowed to delete records from the temporary table.

Deleted records cannot be recovered.

To delete a record from the Temporary Table:

  1. In the Temporary Table, search for and mark the record to be deleted and then click on "Delete Marked"

  1. A confirmation dialog for the delete action displays. Click Ok to delete the record or click Cancel to cancel the deletion.

  1. A message informing that the record has been deleted successfully displays. Click Done.
