Registering Components

To register and store submitted records in the permanent table.

  1. Log in to CBOE with Registrar privileges
  2. Click on Review Register icon under Registration to display the submitted records.
  3. To register multiple records at once, check the Marked checkbox corresponding to the records that you want to register and then click on Register Marked link

  1. Alternatively, to register records one by one, click on Review Record corresponding to each record, and then click on Register button at the top of the window

  1. The component is registered and the following confirmation dialog displays. Click on Done button to complete the registration process.
