Working with Registration Enterprise

Registration Enterprise lets you track and characterize components and batches procured or synthesized by chemical laboratories. When you submit components into Registration Enterprise, the components are typically stored in a temporary table.

Normally, the components stored in the temporary table are reviewed before they are registered in the company-wide permanent database. Registration Enterprise assigns unique IDs to the components you register and lets you perform duplicate checking.

Data Loader lets you perform these tasks pertaining to Registration Enterprise:

  • Load substances
  • Update batch data

NOTE: Batch refers to a collection of components that are essentially the same, but have slightly different properties. For example, you can create batches for the components that are essentially the same, but contain different impurities, behave differently in tests, or are procured from different suppliers.
