Registration Relationships

The following diagram and table describes the relationships between a submitter, supervisor and registrar during the registration process.

Since Submitter A has Supervisor A assigned to him/her and Submitter B has Supervisor B assigned to him/her, the following privileges and limitations will be considered:

  Can edit Records submitted by Submitter A? Can edit Records submitted by Submitter B?
Submitter A Yes No
Submitter B No Yes
Supervisor A Yes No
Supervisor B No Yes
Registrar Yes Yes

The EDIT_SCOPE_ALL privilege overrides everything and allows to edit anybody's records.

The Supervisee can not edit/delete his/her supervisor's records. Submitter A/Submitter B can not edit the Supervisor A/Supervisor B records respectively.
