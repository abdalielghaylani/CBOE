Registering a Compound

After a compound is registered temporarily, you can as a registrar review, register and store submitted records in the permanent table.

If you have a large list in the temporary table, you can run queries to look for a specific record to register.

Once you find the compound to register:

  1. Click on the Detail View icon

The compound's information page displays:

  1. Click on Edit button to make updates if required and save
  2. Click Register button, the compound gets registered permanently and added to the Registration Records table.
