Approving and Registering a Compound

If the approval workflow is enabled, after a submitter submits a component, the record is stored in the temporary database waiting for the approval.

The reviewer has adequate privileges to approve the submitted records. Without approving a record the Registrar will not be able to register a record and store it in the permanent database.

To approve a submitted record:

  1. Log in to Web Registration, the Temporary Registration Records page displays
  2. In the table, click on the Detail view icon corresponding to the record that you want to approve. To quickly find a record, you can run a query by clicking on New Query button.

After you click on the Detail view icon, the following page displays:

  1. Click Delete button to delete the record.
  2. Click Edit button if you want to make edits to the record's information. After you finish editing click Save.
  3. Click Approve button. The record is now approved and ready to be registered. The following page displays:

Note: You can approve a compound from the Temporary Registration Records page, by marking the record and clicking on Approve Marked button as shown in the screen shot below:

  1. Click Cancel Approval for any required updates. Please note that you can cancel the approval from the compound's detail view only, this option is not available in the Temporary Registration Records page.
  2. Click Register. The compound gets registered and a unique registry number gets created automatically. The record gets added to the Registration Records table.
