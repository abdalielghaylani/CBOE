Making a private form public

To make a private form public:
1. Open the private form.
2. Save the form as a public form.
After you change the status from private to public and save the form. The private form still exists. To remove the private form, delete it from the User Forms list.

Closing a Form

To close a form:
1. Select the form tab.
2. Go to File>Close.

Deleting a Form

To delete a form, right-click the form in either the Public Forms or User Forms list and click Delete Form.

Renaming a Form

To rename a form:
1. Right-click the form in either the Public Forms or User Forms list and click Rename Form.
2. Enter a new name for the form and click OK.
