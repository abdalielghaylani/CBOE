Combi Chem Bulk Registration and Bulk Container Creation

Bulk registration and Bulk Container creation are configured for Combi Chem Experiments. Products from combinatorial synthesis are used for bulk registration and bulk container creation.

Please refer to the "Using MPS for E-Notebook" section within the E-Notebook user's guide for further details about the combi chem workflow and the enumeration process.

After enumerating a MPS reaction, perform the below steps:

  1. Navigate to the "MPS Summary" section, products and associated data from combinatorial synthesis are displayed. Select the products to send to the "MPS Registry Form" section and click on "Copy to Registry Form" form tool

  1. The following confirmation dialog displays. Click on Yes button

  1. The selected rows will be copied to the target table of the “MPS Registry form” section
  2. Select the Project, Scientist and Prefix from the drop-down list and enter a Synthesis date
