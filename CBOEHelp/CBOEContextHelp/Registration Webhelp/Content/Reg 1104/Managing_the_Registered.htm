Managing the Registered Records

 
When a record is registered to the permanent table, it will be registered as a batch. Users can then search, edit, and add batches to the registered records. Once a batch is added, it is possible to make edits, move or delete the batches added.
 
Registration allows users to search for records based on any database field. Make your search criteria as specific as possible to minimize the list of search results. Alternatively, try broadening your search criteria to locate the records you want.
 
After searching, mark the search results for later review. See "Marking Records" for information.
