Print Barcode - NSM

The Print Barcode Method gathers information from the Inventory form and creates a barcode label.

Note: To print a barcode label, the Container Barcode parameter should not be empty.

To print a barcode label:

  1. From the Reaction W COE Integration section, click the Send to Inventory form tool and select Open Inventory Form. A dialog appears to select a molecule for printing barcode.
  2. Select the desired molecule and click OK. An Inventory form displays the data associated with the corresponding molecule.
  3. Click the Inventory form tool and select Print Barcode from the menu.

An Internet browser appears.

  1. Label Type: Select a label template from the drop down menu.

The label displays in the window, as shown in the example below.

  1. Click the Printer on the bottom of the toolbar. The Print dialog displays.
  2. Select the printer and click Print.

  1. Save the report to a location on your local machine.

Based on your printer choice, the barcode is printed. In the example below, the "Adobe PDF" printer is selected.
