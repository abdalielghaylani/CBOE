ChemBioOffice Enterprise

The ChemBioOffice Enterprise 12 Suite of applications allows users to record, manage and share wide variety of R&D information. Diverse types of data are displayed on electronic “pages” in E-Notebook and other applications. Users can retrieve information from an extensive set of databases with an easy-to-use Web-based interface.

This Guide provides a detailed explanation of the CBOE 12 applications, their use and functionality.

Please note that not all of the users have the same privileges and access to all of the CBOE applications. The user's access depends upon the company requirements and individual user role(s).
