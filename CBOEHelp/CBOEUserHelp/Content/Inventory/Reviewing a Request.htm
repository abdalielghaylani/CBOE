Reviewing a Request

You can filter your search for submitted compounds and mixtures using the status code, Not Set. After you locate your request, from Tasks > Manage Sample Requests, you can edit your request for compounds or mixtures. Once completed, you can change the approval status code to Approved, and save the record.

To review a request:

  1. Login to CBOE with Reviewer privileges.
  2. From Inventory Enterprise, click Tasks.

  1. Click Manage Sample Requests.

  1. The Manage Sample Requests dialog displays.

  1. Click Review to review, edit, or delete the request. From the Review dialog, you can add a proof of approval document.
  2. Click OK.
