Registration

Welcome to the Registration chapter.

Registration is a ChemBioOffice Enterprise application that allows users to track and characterize the compounds and batches procured or synthesized by the chemical laboratories. It ensures the uniqueness of the compounds by assigning unique IDs to the compounds and providing provisions for automatic duplicate checking.

The current chapter provides you information on how to configure Registration application.

Note: You can find the Registration schema logical model at the same location where CBOE 12.5.2 documentation goods are. This has an Entity Relationship Diagram (ERD) illustrating logical structure of the registration database schema and shows entities, attributes and the relationships between tables within that schema.
