Mapping between ASP cfserver.ini and .Net config.xml

 

In general, the section maps to the equivalent section, for example, Global section maps to Global section.

In general, the flags in cfserver.ini has similar name in config.xml, except underscores are removed from the setting names. Also from all upper case to camel case.

A few renamed settings are as follows:

[REGISTRATION]

ADD_BATCH_FROM_REG_RESULTS  -- <registration><automaticAddFirstBatch></registration>

ALLOW_BATCH_FOR_UNAPPROVED_CMPD -- <registration><allowBatchForUnapprovedCompound></registration>

 

A few newly added settings:

<registration><crateCompoundReviewRequired>

                    <updateCompoundReviewRequired>

                    <createBatchReviewRequired>

                    <updateBatchReviewRequired>

</registration>
