Getting Started

To submit or search for a compound in Registration Enterprise, you must first log into the ChemBioOffice Enterprise Manager home page.

Logging in

To open the Registration Enterprise home page:
1. Enter your user name and password in the ChemBioOffice Enterprise login page and click Log in.
2. In the ChemBioOffice Entereprise Manager home page, click Main Menu under Registration Enterprise. The home page of Registration Enterprise appears.

 

The main menu

Once logged in, you can:
Add compounds to the temporary table.
Review records in the temporary table.
Search the temporary table.
Register a record.
Search for a registered record.

Logging out

You need to log out of Registration Enterprise when you are done submitting, reviewing or registering records.
To log out, click Logout in the top right hand side of the screen.
